Our Goals

What is Our Mission & Vision ?

Vision

To create the world’s first permanent, all-in-one destination that integrates elite beach sports, a wave pool surf circuit, water park attractions, athlete training academies, and health & wellness retreats — positioned in the heart of Bali’s global tourism zone.

Mission

To revolutionise global beach and surf sport tourism by building an iconic, world-class venue that serves athletes, tourists, youth programs, and wellness travellers alike. Our goal is to combine competition, recreation, lifestyle, and development into one unforgettable destination.

Core Facilities

Beach Sports Arena

The facility features undercover and outdoor courts for tennis, paddle, pickleball, beach sports, plus multi-use sand courts.

Surf Circuit / Wave Pool

The Surf Circuit features URBNSURF wave machines, recreational and professional surf lanes, catering to all skill levels and fun seekers.

Water Park

The Water Park offers a lazy river, thrilling slides, kids zones, spectator areas, and comfortable family lounges for everyone & join us .

Retreat & Accommodation

Retreat & Accommodation Zones include wellness retreats, yoga platforms, athlete dorms, and VIP villas for relaxation and comfort.

Training & Coaching Academies

It's offer pro coaching, youth camps, and international programs to develop athletes across various sports.

Events & Competitions

It's host international tournaments with spectator facilities, live broadcasting, and partnerships attracting global athletes year-round.
